CUM_MATRIX

(Champaign-Urbana Mass Transit District + LED Matrix)

CUM-MATRIX combines the CUMTD API,
a 64x32 LED matrix, and a Raspberry Pi to make a mini timetable of CUMTD bus departures. The board refreshes every 30 seconds, and cycles between two screens with two times each -- similar to a real timetable.
