Magritte Bot

magritte_bot was a Twitter bot that generated and Tweeted random alternative versions of René Magritte's 1929 painting The Treachery of Images. The bot picks a random noun from a list, then downloads the first Google Images result for the noun that is a square PNG. Using the Pillow and Tweepy libraries, the image is assembled and spit out on Twitter. The bot was written in Python, and tweeted twice a day per a cronjob on a Raspberry Pi in my dorm.
